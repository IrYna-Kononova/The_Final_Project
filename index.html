Homelessness in Berlin:
a guide to how each of us can help

Homelessness is a complex social issue that involves individuals or families lacking a fixed, regular, and adequate nighttime residence. This problem affects diverse populations, including men, women, children, veterans, individuals with mental health challenges, and those struggling with substance abuse.

Since 2014, there has been a 150% increase in the homeless population within the country. Berlin has some of the highest levels of homelessness in Germany.
